1. Getting Logged in to your computer

  • When you first get to your computer, the first screen you will see is the lock screen.
  • You will need to press CTRL + ALT + DELETE in order to bring up the login screen.
  • Type in your username and password from above.
    • capitilization does not matter for your username
  • You will be prompted to change your password
    • Your password must contain the following:
      • At least 12 characters
      • three of the following four items
        • A capital letter
        • A lowercase letter
        • A number
        • A special character !@#$()>%^&*[]{}<>
    • Your password cannot contain any of the following:
      • Any part of your name
      • Any part of the company name
      • Elkader or Iowa
      • Any common phrases like "password" or "p@ssw0rd"
  • It may take some time to log you in the first time on a new computer.
  • Congrats you are logged in and ready to proceed

  • Click Next
  • Type in the password that you changed your temporary password to
  • You will be prompted to setup MFA next

Setting up MFA

After you log in for the first time you will be told that your organization needs more information. This is the setup process for completing the MFA process.

  • The first method is an APP that needs to be installed on a smart device. We recommend installing this on your smart phone.
    • This app has no permissions to view anything on your phone, and the company is not able to see any data on your phone.
  • Follow the instructions in your web browser for how to setup the app.
  • After installing the app and setting up the first method, it will ask you for a second method.
    • This form can be either a text message or an email address
    • If you choose email, you can not use a work email

After you setup MFA you will be greeted with the Microsoft 365 Portal. From here you can access all the web apps that your license allows. Examples are Outlook, Word, Powerpoint or OneDrive. You can also access SharePoint sites that you have access to as well.

Cybersecurity Training

We have been doing cybersecurity training since the beginning of 2022. We have recently (September 2022) decided that this is is now manadatory for all employees that will use a computer. This training takes 20-30 minutes to complete, and has proven to be very valuable and reduces compromise.

To take the training please follow these instructions:

  • Open Edge
  • go to training.knowbe4.com
  • Enter in your username
    • {{ employee.user_name }}@{{ employee.domain }}
  • From here you can start your training
  • You can also click on Library at the top and take any of the optional training that is available
    • This training has information on other topics in more depth than the required training

When you are done with the training, there is no need to tell the IT department, we are able to see who has and hasn't done their training on our end.
